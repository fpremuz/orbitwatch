# Battery Voltage Failure

## Symptoms

- Battery voltage below 2.5V
- Frequent low power alerts
- Sudden telemetry interruptions
- Reduced satellite uptime

## Possible Causes

- Battery degradation
- Solar panel inefficiency
- Charging subsystem malfunction
- Excessive power consumption

## Risk Level

High

## Recommended Actions

1. Switch satellite to low power mode.
2. Disable non-essential subsystems.
3. Verify solar charging efficiency.
4. Schedule battery diagnostics.
