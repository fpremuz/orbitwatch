# Thermal Anomaly

## Symptoms

- Temperature exceeds 80C
- Repeated thermal alerts
- Sudden component shutdowns

## Possible Causes

- Cooling system failure
- Excessive solar exposure
- Sensor malfunction
- High computational workload

## Risk Level

High

## Recommended Actions

1. Reduce onboard workload.
2. Rotate satellite if possible.
3. Verify thermal sensor accuracy.
4. Monitor temperature trend.
